Key Findings for Mature Lithosphere:
- Mature oceanic lithosphere (70-120 Ma) is generally cool
- Carbonatite melting requires additional heat sources or fluid flux
- CO2-rich fluids can lower solidus temperatures significantly
- Melting may occur only in specific tectonic settings
